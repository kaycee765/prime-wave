# PrimeWave - Zero-Knowledge Academic Credential Verification

## Project Description

PrimeWave revolutionizes academic credential verification by combining blockchain technology with zero-knowledge proofs to create a tamper-proof, privacy-preserving system for digital diplomas and certificates. Built on the Stacks blockchain, this platform empowers educational institutions to issue cryptographically signed NFT-based credentials while enabling students to maintain complete control over their academic records and personal information disclosure.

The platform addresses critical challenges in the current credential verification ecosystem, including rampant degree fraud, time-consuming verification processes, and privacy concerns surrounding sensitive academic data. Through its innovative three-layer architecture, PrimeWave creates a trustless environment where students can prove their qualifications to employers or institutions without exposing unnecessary personal details, while institutions are economically incentivized to maintain educational quality through a reputation staking mechanism.

## Key Features

• **Zero-Knowledge Credential Verification** - Students can prove specific academic achievements like degree completion, field of study, or performance thresholds without revealing exact grades, attendance records, or other sensitive information through advanced zk-SNARK implementations.

• **NFT-Based Digital Diplomas** - Educational institutions mint tamper-proof credential NFTs with embedded cryptographic proofs, creating permanent, verifiable academic records that students own in perpetuity across institutional boundaries.

• **Ring Signature Privacy Protection** - The verification layer utilizes ring signatures to allow credential holders to prove membership in graduate cohorts without revealing individual identity, ensuring anonymity while maintaining verification integrity.

• **Reputation Staking System** - Institutions stake STX tokens based on their graduates' employment outcomes, creating economic incentives for educational quality and generating credibility scores through anonymous outcome data aggregation.

• **Selective Disclosure Protocols** - Students create custom verification packages proving specific claims like graduation date ranges or achievement categories, maintaining privacy boundaries while satisfying verification requirements.

• **Instant Verification Infrastructure** - Employers verify credentials through QR code scanning or API integration, receiving immediate cryptographic proof of authenticity without compromising student privacy or requiring lengthy institutional contact.

• **Merkle Tree Batch Processing** - The smart contract system efficiently handles large-scale credential issuance through Merkle tree commitments, enabling institutions to mint multiple credentials simultaneously while maintaining individual verification capabilities.
